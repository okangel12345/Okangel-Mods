# Okangel's Ultimate Spider-Man

Amazing, spectacular, sensational... ? What about ULTIMATE? This mod brings a suit inspired by Bagley's USM to the game. With 4K textures for each part of the body and those really big lenses, it also has working lookgroups so no weird head issues in cinematics! This mod replaces the NWH red and blue suit.

**Features:**
- USM suit with 4K textures
- Custom icon in the suits menu
- (Optional) Black outline

**Requirements:**
1. Overstrike (1.5.0) https://github.com/Tkachov/Overstrike.
2. Please, make a backup of your "toc" file. I won't be responsible if it gets corrupted!
